My love my life

Who are we? We are a young organic company, founded in 2016. Our start-up is located in Austria and distributes from there under the name Hale GmbH vegan and organic products. Our company name is a fusion of the names of the founders Hackenbuchner and Leeb and also means healthy and vigorous. Our vision Our vision is to create and offer vegan and organic products out of the love for humankind and life itself. Our mission We want to provide sensible people who have their heart in the right place with simply delicious vegan organic products in order to do themselves and the environment a bit of good. Our products should enrich the lifestyle and simplify a balanced diet..
